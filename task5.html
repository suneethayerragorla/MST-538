Angela's recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingrediants

For the dough

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tableaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup better

    • Instructions

    1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar,eggs.
    2. Melt the butter and ad to the mixture.
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combinesf into a dough.
    4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
    5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewehere.
